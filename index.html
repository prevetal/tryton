TryTON Wallet
A semi-custodial cryptocurrency wallet integrated with Telegram, providing users with convenient asset management right through messenger.

The wallet supports leading blockchains - TON, Solana, Ethereum, BSC and plays the role of a link for all TryTON ecosystem products: AirDrop games, NFT collections and interconnect bridges.

Listing

TRT listing on leading blockchains and bridges TRT token will be listed on TON, Solana, Ethereum and BSC (CEX) bridged blockchains to expand financial opportunities and attract users from different networks. This will expand access to the token, increase liquidity and strengthen its market position. Benefits of listing: Increased issuance and liquidity:A staggered listing on new blockchains helps control token issuance, ensuring a stable economy. Airdrop support: 10% of listings are directed to Airdrop, providing players with tangible bonuses. Exchange rate stability: Bridges equalize the TRT exchange rate on different networks, compensating for fluctuations and maintaining stability.

NFT Mint
We have developed and tested parallel mint technology on our own project and are now building a service to allow other projects to release NFT collections through our application or integrate via API.

Benefits of Parallel Minting:
Scalability and speed: Each NFT is created on the user's wallet by a separate contract, allowing NFTs to be released without delay even during high activity.

Commission on the user:
The minting commission falls on the user, simplifying large-scale campaigns and reducing project costs.

Optimization for mass campaigns:
The small commission per NFT is low-noticeable, but allows for costeffective mass marketing campaigns.
